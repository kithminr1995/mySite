I'm a Junior Lecturer at the Department of Electronic and Telecommunication Engineering ( ENTC ), Faculty of Engineering, University of Moratuwa , Sri Lanka. I also work as a Biomedical Researcher, involved in many interesting projects the areas of Bio-signal Processing, Machine Learning and Biomedical Device Design.

I received my Bachelor’s Degree from University of Moratuwa in 2020, where I completed my thesis on “Designing a Cost-Effective Dry Contact sEMG Sensor System for Controlling a Bionic Hand” with Dr. Simon Lind Kappel and Dr. Thilina Dulantha Lalitharatne .

I completed a Research Internship in the Artificial Intelligence R&D team at Telexistence Inc. , Tokyo, Japan under the supervision of Telexistence Co-founder & CTO Dr. Charith Fernando. I focused on applying computer vision and deep learning to develop a technique to detect different types of supermarket beverages using the Telexistence Robot's stereo vision camera. My work was showcased in a video which was produced by the Prime Minister’s Oﬀice of Japan, on how innovative technology will change the way people live and work in Japan.

I am interested in the areas of Biomedical Engineering, Bio-signal Processing, Neurotechnology, Point-of-Care Diagnosis, Systems Neuroscience.

The design projects and research work I have been involved in have been recognized at prestigous international events such as IEEE CASS COVID-19 Special Student Design Competition 2020, IEEE ComSoc Student Competition 2019, and Intel InnovateFPGA 2018 as well as have been covered by local and international press. Further, I was a member of the Sri Lankan Undergraduate team which took part in ABU Robocon 2019, Mongolia and Techkriti IARC 2018, India.
